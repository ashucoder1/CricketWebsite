Suryakumar Yadav

SKY

Suryakumar Yadav, known for his innovative shot-making and 360-degree batting style, has quickly become a fan favorite in T20 cricket. His ability to dominate bowlers and score runs at a rapid pace has earned him a crucial role in the Indian middle order. SKY’s rise in international cricket has been nothing short of spectacular, showcasing his class and adaptability across formats.

Jasprit Bumrah

Jasprit Bumrah is widely regarded as one of the best fast bowlers in the world, known for his deadly yorkers and unorthodox action. His ability to deliver under pressure, especially in death overs, makes him a match-winner for India in all formats of the game. Bumrah’s consistency and skill have made him a cornerstone of the Indian bowling attack, earning praise from cricketing legends worldwide.

Virat Kohli

A modern-day cricketing legend, Virat Kohli is known for his aggressive batting, remarkable consistency, and fierce competitiveness. He has set numerous records, including being the fastest to 10,000 runs in ODIs, and is one of the best chasers in cricket history. Kohli’s passion and dedication to fitness have set new standards in the game, inspiring the next generation of cricketers

Rohit Sharma

Known as the "Hitman" of Indian cricket, Rohit Sharma is celebrated for his elegant stroke play and ability to score big runs. He is the only player to have scored three double centuries in ODIs, making him one of the most destructive openers in the game. Rohit’s leadership skills have been key to Mumbai Indians’ success in the IPL, where he has led the team to multiple titles.
